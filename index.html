Complaints Policy

We are committed to handling all complaints in a fair, transparent, and timely manner. If you have a complaint regarding our car park services, please contact us using the details below.

Data Protection Policy

We take data protection seriously. Our ANPR systems collect vehicle registration data for the purpose of managing car park access and enforcing parking rules.

This data is processed in accordance with the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018. For more information or to request access to your data, please contact our Data Protection Officer.
